Fokoro

Computer Game (ongoing)

Fokoro or The Owl is an indie 2D action-adventure platformer inspired by Japanese culture, aesthetics, and folklore. The player character is a samurai who has the power to transform into a barn owl. The samurai uses his powers to fight enemeis including wild animals, yokais, warlords, spirits, and so on. Fokoro has been critically acclaimed for its in-game animations.

Winter Dreams

Virtual Reality (2020)

Winter Dreams is an independent virtual reality experience created completely in and for VR. It was made using VR technologies such as Oculus QuillVR and Unity 3D. This immersive experience can be viewed on Quill Theater using any VR headsets such as Oculus Rift S and Oculus Quest as well as HTC Vive and HTC Cosmos.

The Third Script

Animation (2017)

The Third Script is a 2D hand-drawn animated short inspired by the fluidity and the sense of motion that is imbued in the art of Persian (Iranian) calligraphy. The animation, which is made entirely out of letters, words and pseudoscript, draws on the symbolic and metaphoric role of the alphabet in Persian mystically-inclined poetry.

Passion of Love

Projection Mapping Installation (2016)

The Passion of Love is a new media installation based on a poem by the Classical Persian poet Rumi (1207-1273 CE). The installation is a video projection mapping on a calligraphic sculpture. This installation is based on my research on transformation of the function of letters and words in calligraphic art.

Muqarnas

Immersive Experience (2018)

Muqarnas is an immersive Dome experience that was inspired by muqarnas (geometric vaultings that are typically applied to the undersides of domes in Islamic architecture.) This immersive film is designed to be experienced on full-domes.

The Boarding House

Animation (2013)

The Boarding House is a short animated film about hope. The film impersonifies a plant that goes through a journey in a modern metropolitan landscape. Train, journey, urban landscape, clouds, and sky all play a significant role in this animated short. The role of landscape in the animation is generally reflected on sticking specific events or moments temporally in the memory by envisaging the sky and clouds.

Cosmic Symphony

Audio-visual Experiment (2013)

Cosmic Symphony is an experimental video that is based on the data received by Radio Telescopes from outer space. The original sounds are from the National Radio Astronomy Observatory (NRAO) that are digitally mixed and manipulated. The images are digitally enhanced real footage recorded on telescopes and amateur space photography.

Symphony No. 40

Animation (2010)

Symphony No. 40 is a 2D hand-drawn visual music animated piece based on one of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart's masterpieces, Symphony No.40 in G minor. The story of the animated visual music piece is about a curious creature who wanders into the world of some other fantasy creatures. He finds himself in trouble as he tries to discover them.
